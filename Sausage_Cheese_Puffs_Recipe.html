Sausage Cheese Puffs

Oven Temp: 400°F

Time: 10–12 minutes

Serves: Depends on size

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. sausage meat
  • 3 cups biscuit mix
  • 1 lb. shredded mild cheddar cheese
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne

Instructions:

  1. Brown sausage meat in skillet, breaking up with fork. Drain off fat. Place in large bowl and cool completely.
  2. Mix in cayenne. Add cheese, biscuit mix, and water; mix with a fork until blended.
  3. Roll into 1-inch balls. (At this point, they can be frozen.)
  4. Place on cookie sheet 2 inches apart.
  5. Bake in 400°F oven for 10–12 minutes.

...and serve it with love.
