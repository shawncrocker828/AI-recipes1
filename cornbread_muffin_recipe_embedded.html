Cornbread Muffin Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (120g) cornmeal
  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk (or buttermilk for extra tang)
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 large egg

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F (200C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease the cups.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, oil (or butter), and egg.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir just until combined; the batter should be slightly lumpy. Do not overmix.
  5. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling them about 3/4 full.
  6. Bake for 1518 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let the muffins cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack. Serve warm with butter and honey if desired.
