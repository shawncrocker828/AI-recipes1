Cheese Ball Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. cream cheese (soft)
  • 3 cups cheese, grated (cold)
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion
  • Dash of Tabasco sauce
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • Dash of Worcestershire sauce
  • Pecans

Instructions

Blend all ingredients together and shape into a ball. Roll in chopped nuts. Wrap in foil and chill. Serve as a spread for crackers.
