Chili Dogs Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 cup tomato juice
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 8 hot dogs, cooked
  • 8 hot dog buns
  • Chopped onion and shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Instructions

In a large skillet, brown beef and garlic; drain. Stir in the next six ingredients. Simmer, uncovered, for 20 minutes. Serve.
