Homemade Biscuit Mix Recipe

Ingredients for the Mix:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter or shortening (cold, cut into small cubes)

To Make the Biscuits (per 1 cup of mix):

  • 1 cup biscuit mix
  • 1/3 to 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine 1 cup of biscuit mix with 1/3 to 1/2 cup milk. Stir until just combined.
  3. Turn the dough onto a floured surface, knead gently, and pat to 1/2-inch thickness.
  4. Cut out biscuits and place on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
